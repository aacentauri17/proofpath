    How it works

    From scattered proof to a clear plan.

    ProofPath turns what you have already done into a readiness score, a shareable profile, and the exact next steps - no guessing, no cost.

    • 1. Self-audit: add your degree, target role, and the proof you already have. It takes about two minutes.
    • 2. Get your score: see your internship readiness, a clear breakdown, and the single biggest gap holding you back.
    • 3. Build visible proof: follow a 14-day plan and a ranked certificate list, each paired with the proof to post afterwards.
    • 4. Share it: copy your proof profile or share a link on LinkedIn and WhatsApp so recruiters see proof, not just claims.
